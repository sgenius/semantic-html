A subtitle

The tag pp means "a paragraph". It is not considered semantic, because it can apply to any text regardless of its meaning.

Some facts

  1. The section tag is semantic. Think of a newspaper:
    • "Sports" is a section within it.
    • "Classified ads" is another section.
  2. The ol and li tags are not semantic: they can be used in different contexts and do not carry additional meaning.
  3. You can mark the header and footer of a section - but can be useful.
Remember these facts well! This is the end of the "Some facts" section.

The journal of a tag

November 8, 2023

I was born

Today, my author wrote me for the first time. It is marvelous to exist!

By the way, although strong and em (for "emphasis") mark text, they do not convey a specific meaning and therefore they are not semantic.

November 4, 2023

I was not born yet

Today, my author thought about writing me, but had no time and therefore I was not born.

An "author" is the creator of a text.

Articles can have footers, too!

The figure tag

The figure tag is multifacetic! Think of it as an illustration in a book.

You can also use it to put some example code.

By the way, code is semantic (it means "this is programming code") while "pre" is not (it's just "preformatted text").
